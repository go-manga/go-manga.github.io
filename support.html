送出前Before you send

到 Issues 後請選擇模板Pick the template on Issues

  • 先搜尋既有 issue，避免重複回報。Search existing issues first to avoid duplicates.
  • 按 New issue 後，選 MangaFuru bug report 並照欄位填寫。Hit New issue, choose “MangaFuru bug report,” and fill the fields.
  • 閱讀器問題：附上章節切換、閱讀方向與能否穩定重現。Reader issues: include chapter switching, reading direction, and whether it reproduces.
  • 來源問題：只填公開 host，勿貼 cookie、token 或帳密。Source issues: public host only — never cookies, tokens, or credentials.
  • 診斷日誌：先確認不含私密資料再附上。Diagnostics: confirm there's nothing private before attaching.

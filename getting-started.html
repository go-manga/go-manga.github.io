上手指南Getting started

五步走進 MangaFuruMangaFuru in five steps

這份指南只涵蓋 MangaFuru App 自己負責的流程；來源內容、帳號、授權與可用性，由你設定的來源或服務決定。 This guide covers only what the MangaFuru app itself handles. Source content, accounts, authorization, and availability are determined by the sources or services you configure.

最快的兩條上手路徑，都是你已經擁有的內容：① 匯入本機的 EPUB、PDF、CBZ，原地就能讀；② 連上你自架的 Komga 伺服器，讀你整理好的書庫。想要更多時，再依下面的步驟自行設定其他來源。 The two fastest on-ramps are content you already own: ① import local EPUB, PDF, and CBZ files and read them in place; ② connect to your own self-hosted Komga server and read the library you organized. When you want more, configure other sources yourself using the steps below.

1

先加入 RepositoriesAdd a repository first

MangaFuru 的來源流程從 Repositories 開始。加入 repository 後，App 才能顯示可安裝或可使用的 Sources。MangaFuru's source flow starts with repositories. Once you add one, the app can show the sources available to install or use.

MangaFuru 不提供官方內容目錄，也不推薦特定第三方來源。請只加入你信任且有權使用的 repository 或本機服務。MangaFuru ships no official catalog and recommends no particular third-party source. Add only repositories or local services you trust and have the right to use.

2

安裝或啟用 SourcesInstall or enable sources

Sources 是你用來瀏覽目錄、搜尋、開啟詳情與載入章節的來源入口。若來源需要登入、mirror、token 或驗證，請在 App 內的 Source Settings 依來源要求設定。Sources are how you browse catalogs, search, open details, and load chapters. If a source needs login, a mirror, a token, or verification, configure it in the in-app Source Settings as that source requires.

3

瀏覽、加入書庫或稍後閱讀Browse, then add to library or read-later

從 Browse 或來源 catalog 開啟作品詳情後，你可以加入書庫或稍後閱讀。書庫適合長期追蹤；稍後閱讀適合先保存但還不想放進正式書庫的作品。Open a title from Browse or a source catalog, then add it to your library or read-later. The library suits titles you follow long-term; read-later is for things you want to keep without committing to the main library yet.

4

開始閱讀與續讀Read and resume

從作品詳情、書庫、稍後閱讀或閱讀歷史都可以回到閱讀流程。MangaFuru 會保存閱讀進度，讓你下次從合適的位置繼續。Jump back into reading from a title's detail, your library, read-later, or history. MangaFuru saves your progress so you can pick up from the right place next time.

5

遇到問題時When something breaks

若來源需要驗證、章節空白、圖片載入失敗、進度沒有保存或 App 行為異常，請先記下發生位置、App 版本、iOS 版本、來源 host 與重現步驟，再到 回報問題 建立 issue。If a source needs verification, a chapter comes up blank, images fail to load, progress isn't saved, or the app behaves oddly, note the area, app version, iOS version, source host, and steps to reproduce, then open an issue from Report an issue.

提醒：請不要在公開回報中貼出密碼、私密 token、完整 cookie、付費內容或他人個資。來源相關欄位只填可公開資訊。 Reminder: never paste passwords, private tokens, full cookies, paid content, or other people's personal data into public reports. For source fields, share public information only.

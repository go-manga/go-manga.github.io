This policy explains how data is handled when you use MangaFuru, a local-first iOS manga reader for iOS 18 and later, when you visit our website, and when you report issues to us through GitHub Issues.

2. 我們的核心原則：本機優先、無帳號2. Our Core Principle: Local-First, No Account

MangaFuru 的根本設計原則是本機優先。實務上，這代表：

  • 沒有 MangaFuru 帳號系統。您無需註冊、登入或建立任何帳號即可使用本應用程式。
  • 您的書庫與閱讀資料預設留在您的裝置上。除非您主動提交某些內容（例如問題回報、螢幕截圖或診斷片段），否則您的書庫、閱讀進度與來源設定不會離開您的裝置傳送給我們。
  • 我們不託管、不上傳、不儲存、不銷售、不散布、也不編列漫畫內容索引。MangaFuru 並未隨附任何官方內容目錄或推薦來源清單。

MangaFuru 是一款漫畫閱讀器，外加一個來源設定框架。應用程式僅在您的指示與設定下運作，向您所選擇的來源請求並顯示內容；所有的來源、檔案、帳戶、憑證與存取權限，都是由您自行設定與控制。

The fundamental design principle of MangaFuru is local-first. In practice, this means:

  • There is no MangaFuru account system. You do not need to register, sign in, or create any account to use the app.
  • Your library and reading data stay on your device by default. Unless you actively submit something (for example, an issue report, a screenshot, or a diagnostic snippet), your library, reading progress, and source settings do not leave your device or get sent to us.
  • We do not host, upload, store, sell, distribute, or index manga content. MangaFuru ships no official content catalog and no recommended-source list.

MangaFuru is a manga reader plus a source-configuration framework. Acting solely on your instruction and configuration, the app requests and displays content from the sources you choose; all sources, files, accounts, credentials, and access rights are configured and controlled by you.

3. 儲存在您裝置上的資料3. Data Stored on Your Device

下列資料是在您裝置本機上建立並儲存。在正常使用情況下，這些資料不會傳送給 MangaFuru 團隊：

  • 您的書庫，以及匯入的內容項目；
  • 稍後再看／歷史清單與閱讀紀錄；
  • 閱讀進度（包含章節與頁面位置）；
  • 已安裝的來源紀錄、儲存庫設定與個別來源設定；
  • 應用程式偏好設定（例如語言、外觀、閱讀方向、快取設定）；
  • 封面、頁面影像與中繼資料的快取；
  • 本機診斷紀錄（log）。

這些資料由 iOS 在您裝置上管理。您可以透過刪除個別項目、清除快取，或自您的裝置移除應用程式來刪除這些資料。

本應用程式內建一個 Local（本機）來源，作為一個內容中立的機制，供您匯入或閱讀您在自己裝置或網路上已經擁有並控制的檔案。它本身不隨附任何內容、不編列任何索引，亦非由我們提供或推薦的內容來源。移除這個 Local 來源只會移除其進入點紀錄，而不會刪除您先前匯入的內容。

The following data is created and stored locally on your device. Under normal use, it is not transmitted to the MangaFuru Team:

  • your library and imported content items;
  • read-later / history lists and reading history;
  • reading progress (including chapter and page position);
  • installed-source records, repository configuration, and individual source settings;
  • app preferences (such as language, appearance, reading direction, and cache settings);
  • caches of cover images, page images, and metadata;
  • local diagnostic logs.

This data is managed by iOS on your device. You can delete it by removing individual items, clearing caches, or uninstalling the app from your device.

The app includes a built-in Local source, which is a content-agnostic mechanism that lets you import or read files you already own and control on your own device or network. It ships with no content of its own, indexes nothing, and is not a content source provided or recommended by us. Removing the Local source only removes its entry-point record and does not delete content you previously imported.

我們（MangaFuru 團隊）不收集以下資訊：

我們唯一會收到的資訊，是您主動選擇提交給我們的內容，例如您透過 GitHub Issues 發送的問題回報、螢幕截圖或診斷片段（詳見第 7 節），以及您主動寄送至我們聯絡信箱的電子郵件。在這些情況下，我們僅會處理您所提供的內容，以便理解並回應您的請求。

請注意，當應用程式依您的設定連線至您所選擇的來源時，此類網路請求在技術上必然會將您的裝置 IP 位址及您所請求的內容（例如搜尋字詞）傳送給該第三方或自架來源；同理，當您與我們的網站或 GitHub 互動時，GitHub 作為基礎設施供應商，可能會依其自身政策處理標準的伺服器紀錄、IP 位址與帳號／登入狀態（詳見第 6 節）。上述各項均非由 MangaFuru 團隊收集。

We (the MangaFuru Team) do not collect the following:

  • we do not collect MangaFuru account or login credentials (there is no account system);
  • we do not collect your library, reading progress, history, or source settings;
  • other than the website analytics and advertising described in Section 6 (Website and GitHub), we do not actively collect your behavioral, usage, or device-identifier data through the app;
  • we do not request or maintain personal profiles such as names, email lists, or contacts.

The only information we receive is what you actively choose to submit to us, such as an issue report, a screenshot, or a diagnostic snippet you send through GitHub Issues (see Section 7), and any email you choose to send to our contact address. In those cases, we process only the content you provide, in order to understand and respond to your request.

Note that when the app connects to a source you have chosen, as you have configured it, such network requests necessarily transmit your device's IP address and the content you request (such as search terms) to that third-party or self-hosted source; likewise, when you interact with our website or with GitHub, GitHub, acting as an infrastructure provider, may process standard server logs, IP addresses, and account/login state under its own policies (see Section 6). None of the foregoing is collected by the MangaFuru Team.

5. 來源與網路連線5. Sources and Network Connections

MangaFuru 讓您新增「儲存庫（Repositories）」並安裝或啟用「來源（Sources）」。來源可能是第三方擴充功能、遠端服務，或運行於您自己網路或裝置上的本機服務。應用程式接著會在您的指示與設定下，自這些由您設定的來源瀏覽、搜尋、開啟及顯示內容。

當應用程式連線至某個來源或來源服務時，是依照您的設定進行連線。任何此類連線所傳送的資料（例如搜尋字詞、所請求的內容，或您為該來源輸入的憑證），均依您的指示，傳送至您所選擇的來源。這些第三方或自架服務不在 MangaFuru 團隊的控制範圍內，並依其各自的隱私權政策與服務條款運作。

由於某些來源可能在您的本機網路上運行，或本應用程式本身可能會在您的裝置上啟動一個本機伺服器，因此 iOS 可能會提示您授予「本機網路」權限。此權限是用於與您裝置或本機網路上的本機來源服務通訊，並非用於將您的活動傳送給我們。

您須自行對所設定與存取之來源及內容的合法性與權利負完全責任，並須遵守適用法律與各來源自身的條款。MangaFuru 團隊不推薦任何特定來源，亦不隨附任何精選來源清單；前述內建的 Local 來源僅為一個內容中立的本機機制，並不提供或指向任何內容。

MangaFuru lets you add "Repositories" and install or enable "Sources". A source may be a third-party extension, a remote service, or a local service running on your own network or device. Acting on your instruction and configuration, the app then browses, searches, opens, and displays content from those sources you configure.

When the app connects to a source or source service, it does so as you have configured it. Any data sent over such a connection (for example, search terms, the content requested, or credentials you enter for that source) is sent, at your direction, to the source you have chosen. These third-party or self-hosted services are outside the MangaFuru Team's control and operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

Because some sources may run on your local network, or because the app itself may host a local server on your device, iOS may prompt you to grant Local Network permission. This permission is used to communicate with local source services on your device or local network, not to transmit your activity to us.

You are solely responsible for the legality and rights of whatever sources and content you configure and access, and for complying with applicable law and each source's own terms. The MangaFuru Team recommends no particular source and ships no curated source list; the built-in Local source described above is only a content-agnostic local mechanism and provides or points to no content.

Important security warning: do not submit passwords, private tokens, full cookies, paid content, personal data, or other secrets in public reports or diagnostics. Before submitting a screenshot or log, remove any sensitive or personally identifiable information.

8. 法律依據與您的隱私權利8. Legal Bases and Your Privacy Rights

由於 MangaFuru 採本機優先設計，我們所持有的個人資料極少或近乎沒有。我們唯一實際進行的處理，是處理您主動提交的內容（主要為問題回報與支援電子郵件）。就此處理，我們的法律依據為：為回應您的請求並維護應用程式之正當利益。在您主動提交內容構成同意之情形下，該同意亦可作為依據。

視適用法律而定（例如 GDPR、UK GDPR、CCPA／CPRA 或類似法規），且於符合各該權利之適用條件時，您可能就我們所持有的任何個人資料享有下列權利：

  • 存取我們所持有關於您的個人資料；
  • 更正不正確或不完整的資料；
  • 刪除您的資料（「被遺忘權」）；
  • 反對基於正當利益所進行之處理（GDPR 第 21 條）；
  • 限制特定處理行為；
  • 在技術可行且符合適用條件時，以可攜格式取得您的資料。

常見問題Frequently asked

關於來源、內容、資料與回報的邊界，這裡有最直接的答案。 The most direct answers on sources, content, data, and reporting boundaries.

我可以讀自己的 EPUB、PDF、CBZ 檔案嗎？Can I read my own EPUB, PDF, and CBZ files?

可以。MangaFuru 內建本機閱讀，能直接開啟你裝置或本機網路上的 EPUB、PDF 與 CBZ，並保留續讀進度。這些是你自己提供的檔案，App 不代管也不散佈任何內容。Yes. MangaFuru includes local reading and can open EPUB, PDF, and CBZ files on your device or local network, with resume preserved. These are files you supply yourself — the app hosts and distributes no content.

MangaFuru 可以連我自架的 Komga 伺服器嗎？Does MangaFuru work with my own Komga server?

可以。MangaFuru 能連到你自行架設的 Komga 伺服器，閱讀你整理在上面的書庫。Komga 由你自己部署與管理，MangaFuru 僅作為讀取端，不託管你的伺服器或內容。Yes. MangaFuru can connect to a Komga server you host yourself and read the library you organized there. You deploy and manage Komga; MangaFuru acts only as the reader and hosts neither your server nor its content.

MangaFuru 會提供漫畫內容或來源清單嗎？Does MangaFuru provide manga content or a list of sources?

不會。MangaFuru 是閱讀器與來源設定框架，不代管、不販售、不散佈漫畫內容，也不提供官方內容目錄。來源、檔案與內容由你自行設定與管理。No. MangaFuru is a reader and a source-configuration framework. It does not host, sell, or distribute manga content, and ships no official catalog. Sources, files, and content are yours to configure and manage.

為什麼需要 Repositories 和 Sources？Why are there both repositories and sources?

Repositories 是來源資訊的入口；Sources 是實際瀏覽、搜尋、開啟詳情與載入章節的來源。這個流程讓 App 可以把來源設定、書庫、清單與閱讀器分開管理。A repository is the entry point for source information; a source is what actually browses, searches, opens details, and loads chapters. Splitting them lets the app manage source setup, library, lists, and the reader separately.

MangaFuru 可以保證某個來源一定可用嗎？Can MangaFuru guarantee a given source keeps working?

不能。第三方來源可能改版、下線、要求登入、觸發驗證、限制地區或修改使用條款。MangaFuru 可以協助把 App 端問題修好，但無法控制第三方來源。No. Third-party sources can change, go offline, require login, trigger verification, restrict regions, or change their terms. MangaFuru can help fix app-side problems, but it has no control over third-party sources.

我該如何回報 bug？How do I report a bug?

請使用 回報問題，提供發生位置、App 版本、iOS 版本、裝置、來源 host、重現步驟、預期結果與實際結果。請不要貼完整 cookie、token、帳密或付費內容。Use Report an issue, with the area, app version, iOS version, device, source host, steps to reproduce, expected result, and actual result. Do not paste full cookies, tokens, credentials, or paid content.

診斷日誌可以公開貼上嗎？Can I post diagnostic logs publicly?

請先檢查內容。若包含私密 token、完整 cookie、帳號資訊、個人資料或付費內容，請不要公開提交。公開 issue 可能被其他人看見。Review them first. If they contain private tokens, full cookies, account details, personal data, or paid content, do not post them publicly. Public issues can be seen by anyone.

網站會用分析或廣告嗎？Does the website use analytics or ads?

網站可能使用網站分析（例如 Google Analytics）並顯示廣告（例如 Google 廣告），這類第三方服務可能設定 Cookie 或收集使用資料。在需要事前同意的地區，我們會於啟用前提供 Cookie 同意機制。詳情請見隱私權政策。網站由 GitHub Pages 提供，GitHub 仍可能依自身政策處理標準伺服器記錄與登入狀態。應用程式內的書庫與閱讀資料則主要保存在你的裝置上。The website may use web analytics (such as Google Analytics) and display ads (such as Google advertising); these third-party services may set cookies or collect usage data. Where prior consent is required, we'll present a cookie-consent prompt before enabling them — see the Privacy Policy for details. The site is served from GitHub Pages, and GitHub may still process standard server logs and login state under its own policies. Your in-app library and reading data stay mainly on your device.

我可以要求支援特定來源嗎？Can I request support for a specific source?

可以回報 App 行為問題，但請不要把 issue 當作來源推薦清單或內容索引。若來源本身要求登入、授權或特定設定，請先確認你有權使用。You can report app-behavior problems, but please don't use issues as a source-recommendation list or content index. If a source requires login, authorization, or specific setup, confirm you have the right to use it first.

我的資料存在哪裡？換手機會不見嗎？Where is my data stored? Do I lose it on a new phone?

書庫、進度、清單與偏好主要保存在你的裝置上，沒有 MangaFuru 帳號系統。請自行備份重要設定；重裝、換機或系統異常都可能造成本機資料變動或遺失。Your library, progress, lists, and preferences live mainly on your device — there is no MangaFuru account. Back up anything important yourself; reinstalling, switching devices, or system issues can change or lose local data.
